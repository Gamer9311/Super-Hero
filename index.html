Super Hero!!!

  1. Here is a instruction Guide to Use the App!
  2. Use Arrow Keys To Move!
  3. Click on H to Get a Random Super Heroes Left Hand!
  4. Click on R to Get a Random Super Heroes Right Hand!
  5. Click on B to Get a Random Super Heroes Body!
  6. Click on F to Get a Random Super Heroes Face!
  7. Click on L to Get a Random Super Heroes Legs!
  8. Click on Shift and UP to Increase Starting Size and Shift And DOWN to Decrease Starting Size!
  9. Click on Shift and RIGHT to Increase Starting X AND Y and Shift And LEFT to Decrease Starting X AND Y!
  10. Drag a certain area to create a group!
  11. Drag Parts Out The Workspace to delete them!
  12. Create Your own Super Heroes And ENJOY!
